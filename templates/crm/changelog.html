Changelog

2026-02-10
Version 0.3.0 - Non-stable
  • Lead Tag System: Added structured tags for leads (emails) with a new LeadTag model and Lead.tags Many-to-Many.
  • Bulk actions in /leads: select emails, apply a tag to selected leads, and send selected leads to Mailchimp.
  • Send by tag: send all leads with a chosen tag to Mailchimp for common campaign actions.
  • Search by tags: lead search now matches tag names too.
  • Mailchimp sync: members are upserted and CRM tags are pushed as Mailchimp audience tags.
  • UI/UX Improvements: Compact icon-only action buttons; improved Lead Stage labels; added a Name column to the leads list.
  • Company Notes System: Added per-company notes at /companies/<domain>/notes/ with full CRUD (add/edit/delete), a new CompanyNote model (timestamps), admin registration, and tests.
2026-01-28
Version 0.2.0 – Non-stable
  • UI/UX improvements.
  • Search & Filtering: Company and lead search, filter by name, domain or email.
  • AI Lead Enrichment: AI enrichment during CSV upload, real-time progress, job title/LinkedIn extraction.
  • Automated Lead Scoring: Multi-signal algorithm (session count, adoption, job title, domain, etc.), auto-calculated on save, read-only in UI, management command for bulk updates.
  • Bug Fixes: CSV import button, new lead button, Gemini AI model update, cancel button, search UX, clickable links, non-editable score/stage.
2026-01-19
Version 0.1.0 – Non-stable
  • Initial Django CRM foundation with AI-powered enrichment (DuckDuckGo, Gemini, OpenAI).
  • Comprehensive models for leads and companies, with enrichment and scoring fields.
  • Bulk CSV import, CRUD for leads/companies, admin customizations, template system, and utility scripts.
